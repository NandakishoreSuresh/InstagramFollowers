Instagram Unfollower Checker

Upload your Instagram followers and following HTML files to see who doesn't follow you back.

How to get your HTML files
  1. Go to Instagram → Settings → Accounts Center → Your information and permissions → Download your information.
  2. Choose Some of your information → select Followers and following.
  3. Set format to HTML, request the download, then unzip the file Instagram emails you.
  4. Inside connections/followers_and_following/ upload followers_1.html and following.html below.
  5. Everything runs in your browser — nothing is uploaded anywhere.
